Mystic Crusade is an indie game developed by For Better or For Worse: Indie Games. This work in progress is being built from the ground up. Battle monsters and complete quests in this old-school turn-based RPG.

Mystic Crusade features a classic turn-based battle system. Level up and gain loot by fighting various creatures across the world. There are many weapons at our fighters disposal. Some of our heroes will be better with a blade, while others prefer magic and spells. Of course, the opposition will have it's own devices to combat these heroes.

For Better or For Worse: Indie Games is our new team that wants to build fun and cheap RPGs for the fans of classic video games. Mystic Crusade will be an awesome pilot game for this team. For Better or For Worse: Indie Games has built a framework for RPGs from the ground up and is very excited to get it in the hands of the players. With hard work and a little luck, Mystic Crusade will be successful enough for us to continue making new games that feature classic game mechanics. Please follow For Better or For Worse: Indie Games at our blog.
